About this tool:

This is website made for viewing, adding and editing information about the Center of Academic Excellence(CAE) Programs in your website.

You are currently logged in as an Institution Admin. Your access to this website is limited.

You have access to following:

  1. View for all designated Institutions

  2. View all the User in every Institution

  3. Search for the Program/s from all designated Institutions

  4. View Courses in the Programs

  5. View Transfer Agreements between Institutes

You can also:

  1. Change information about your Institute

  2. Add or Edit Users to Your Institute

  3. Edit Programs to your Institute

  4. Add or Edit Courses in the Programs

  5. Add or Edit Tranfer Agreement between your Institution.
